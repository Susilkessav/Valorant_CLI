# Veto — Sentinel

Empowered by an unstoppable DNA mutation, Senegalese enforcer Veto defies the rules of engagement by nullifying his opponent's powers and technology. On Veto's battlefield, gunplay is your only guarantee.

## Abilities

### [E] Interceptor  (free)

EQUIP the Interceptor. FIRE to place the Interceptor at projected location. Once placed, RE-USE to
activate. Once active, it will destroy any utility that would BOUNCE off a player and/or be
destroyed naturally by gunfire. Enemies can destroy the Interceptor.

### [C] Crosscut  (200 cr)

EQUIP a vortex. FIRE to place on the ground. While in range and looking at the vortex, ACTIVATE to
teleport to the vortex. During the BUY PHASE, the vortex can be reclaimed to be REDEPLOYED.

### [X] Evolution  (6 ult pts)

INSTANTLY begin to fully mutate, gaining a combat stim, regeneration, and becoming IMMUNE to all
forms of debuffs.

### [Q] Chokehold  (150 cr)

EQUIP a viscous fragment of your mutation. FIRE to throw. The fragment deploys upon hitting the
ground, creating a trap to hold enemies in place. Held enemies are Deafened, and Decayed. Enemies
can destroy the trap before activation.

## Playstyle

Anti-utility sentinel. Interceptor hard-counters flash and grenade spam, forcing enemies into pure gunfights. Crosscut provides repositioning to cover multiple angles. Chokehold traps enemies who push your setup.

## Economy Note

Interceptor is free and denies entire ability compositions — always deploy it. Chokehold (150) is a cheap trap; buy it every full round.
