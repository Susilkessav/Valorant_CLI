# Waylay — Duelist

Thailand's prismatic radiant Waylay transforms into light itself as she darts across the battlefield, striking down her targets through shards of light before flitting back to safety, all in the blink of an eye.

## Abilities

### [E] Refract  (free, 1 charge(s))

INSTANTLY create a beacon of light on the floor. REACTIVATE to speed back to your beacon as a mote
of pure light. You are invulnerable as you travel. Refract resets a charge every two kills.

### [C] Saturate  (200 cr)

EQUIP a cluster of light. FIRE to throw the cluster, which upon contact with the ground explodes,
Hindering nearby players with a powerful movement and weapon slow.

### [Q] Lightspeed  (150 cr)

EQUIP to prepare for a burst of speed. FIRE to dash forward twice. ALT FIRE to dash once. Only your
first dash can send you upward.

### [X] Convergent Paths  (6 ult pts)

EQUIP to focus your prismatic power. FIRE to create an afterimage of yourself that projects a beam
of light. After a brief delay, you gain a powerful speed boost and the beam expands, Hindering other
players in the area.

## Playstyle

Mobility-based entry duelist. Refract enables aggressive peeks with a guaranteed escape route. Lightspeed chains upward then horizontal for vertical entry. Saturate slows defenders mid-push.

## Economy Note

Refract is free and refreshes on kills — always keep it active before peeking. Lightspeed (150) is cheap mobility; buy it every full round.
