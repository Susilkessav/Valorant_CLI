# Iso — Duelist

Chinese fixer for hire Iso falls into a flow state to dismantle the opposition. Reconfiguring ambient energy into bulletproof protection, he advances with focus towards his next duel to the death.

## Abilities

### [Q] Undercut  (200 cr)

EQUIP a molecular bolt. FIRE to throw it forward, briefly applying Vulnerable & Suppress to all
players it touches. The bolt can pass through solid objects, including walls.

### [X] Kill Contract  (7 ult pts)

EQUIP an interdimensional arena. FIRE to hurl a column of energy through the battlefield, pulling
and healing both you and the first enemy hit into the arena to duel to the death.

### [E] Double Tap  (free, 1 charge(s))

INSTANTLY start channeling your focus. Once focused: gain a shield which absorbs one instance of
damage from any source, reload more quickly, and enter a flow state during which downed enemies you
kill or damage spawn an energy orb. Shooting this orb refreshes your flow state and your existing
shield, or grants another.

### [C] Contingency  (250 cr)

EQUIP to assemble prismatic energy. FIRE to push an indestructible wall of energy forward that
blocks bullets. ALT FIRE to push out a slower version of the wall.

## Playstyle

Duel-focused duelist. Double Tap rewards trading aggressively. Contingency creates instant linear cover mid-execute.

## Economy Note

Double Tap is free; always activate it before entering site.
