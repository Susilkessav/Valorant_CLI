# Skye — Initiator

Hailing from Australia, Skye and her band of beasts trailblaze the way through hostile territory. With her creations hampering the enemy, and her power to heal others, the team is strongest and safest by Skye's side.

## Abilities

### [Q] Trailblazer  (250 cr)

EQUIP a Tasmanian tiger trinket. FIRE to send out and take control of the predator. While in
control, FIRE to leap forward, exploding in a Concussive blast on impact and damaging directly hit
enemies.

### [E] Guiding Light  (250 cr, 3 charge(s))

EQUIP a hawk trinket. FIRE to send it forward. HOLD FIRE to guide the hawk in the direction of your
crosshair. RE-USE while the hawk is in flight to transform it into a flash. The flash reaches max
potency after a short duration during the hawk's flight.

### [C] Regrowth  (free)

EQUIP a healing trinket. HOLD FIRE to channel, Healing allies in range and line of sight. Can be
reused until her healing pool is depleted. Skye cannot heal herself.

### [X] Seekers  (7 ult pts)

EQUIP a Seeker trinket. FIRE to send out three Seekers to track down the three closest enemies. If a
Seeker reaches its target, it Nearsights them. Enemies can destroy the Seekers.

## Playstyle

Heal + info initiator. 3 charges of Guiding Light are the best flash economy in the game. Trailblazer scouts + concusses corners safely.

## Economy Note

Regrowth is free healing — position near teammates. Guiding Light (250) is the must-buy.
