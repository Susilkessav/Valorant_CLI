# Vyse — Sentinel

Metallic mastermind Vyse unleashes liquid metal to isolate, trap, and disarm her enemies. Through cunning and manipulation, she forces all who oppose her to fear the battlefield itself.

## Abilities

### [Q] Shear  (free)

EQUIP filaments of liquid metal. FIRE to place a hidden wall trap. When an enemy crosses, an
indestructible wall bursts from the ground behind them. The wall lasts for a brief time before
dissipating.

### [E] Arc Rose  (200 cr, 2 charge(s))

EQUIP an Arc Rose. Target a surface and FIRE to place a stealthed Arc Rose, or ALT FIRE to place the
Arc Rose through it. REUSE to blind all players looking at it. This ability can be picked up to be
REDEPLOYED.

### [C] Razorvine  (200 cr)

EQUIP a nest of liquid metal. FIRE to launch. Upon landing, the nest becomes invisible. When
ACTIVATED, it sprawls out into a large razorvine nest which slows and damages all players who move
through it.

### [X] Steel Garden  (7 ult pts)

EQUIP a bramble of liquid metal. FIRE to send the metal erupting from you as a torrent of metal
thorns, JAMMING enemy primary weapons after a brief windup.

## Playstyle

Area-denial sentinel. Arc Roses act as hidden tripwires with a root instead of a slow. Steel Garden can lock down an entire site on a retake.

## Economy Note

Shear is free wall denial. Buy both Arc Roses (400 total) for trap coverage on full-buys.
