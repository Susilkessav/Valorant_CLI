# Yoru — Duelist

Japanese native Yoru rips holes straight through reality to infiltrate enemy lines unseen. Using deception and aggression in equal measure, he gets the drop on each target before they know where to look.

## Abilities

### [C] FAKEOUT  (100 cr, 2 charge(s))

EQUIP an echo that transforms into a mirror image of Yoru when activated. FIRE to instantly activate
the mirror image and send it forward. ALT FIRE to place an inactive echo. USE to transform an
inactive echo into a mirror image and send it forward.  Mirror images explode in a Blinding flash
when destroyed by enemies.

### [Q] BLINDSIDE  (200 cr)

EQUIP to rip an unstable dimensional fragment from reality. FIRE to throw the fragment, activating a
flash that winds up once it collides with a hard surface.

### [E] GATECRASH  (150 cr, 2 charge(s))

EQUIP a rift tether FIRE to send the tether forward. ALT FIRE to place a stationary tether. ACTIVATE
to teleport to the tether's location. USE to trigger a fake teleport. GATECRASH resets a charge
every two kills.

### [X] DIMENSIONAL DRIFT  (7 ult pts)

EQUIP a mask that can see between dimensions. FIRE to drift into Yoru's dimension, unable to be
affected or seen by enemies from the outside. REACTIVATE to exit Yoru's dimension early.

## Playstyle

Deception and flanking duelist. Dimensional Drift enables deep scouting and denying info. High skill ceiling.

## Economy Note

Fakeout (100) is dirt cheap misdirection; always buy both charges.
