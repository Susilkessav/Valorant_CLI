# Miks — Controller

Straight from Croatia, Miks takes the stage channeling pure sound energy. With his infectious passion and sonic powers, he rallies his squad to move as one as they set the tempo on the battlefield together.

## Abilities

### [C] M-pulse  (200 cr)

EQUIP M-pulse. ALT-FIRE to toggle between Concuss and Healing outputs. FIRE to throw the device.
Upon landing, M-pulse sends out sound waves, either Concussing or Healing players.

### [E] Waveform  (free)

EQUIP a Map Targeter. FIRE to set locations. ALT-FIRE to spawn Smokes at selected locations.

### [Q] Harmonize  (250 cr)

EQUIP Harmonize. Target an ally and FIRE to activate a Combat Stim on yourself and the ally that
refreshes with each kill. ALT-FIRE to grant Combat Stim to yourself.

### [X] Bassquake  (8 ult pts)

EQUIP Bassquake. FIRE to build up and unleash Sonic Radiance forward, knocking back, Deafening, and
Slowing players.

## Playstyle

Team-amplifying controller. Waveform provides free smokes every round. M-pulse in Healing mode is the only mid-round area heal in the game. Harmonize stacks with aggressive teammates for combat-stim chains.

## Economy Note

Waveform is free — always place smokes. M-pulse (200) doubles as a cheap concuss or heal depending on the situation.
