# Tejo — Initiator

A veteran intelligence consultant from Colombia, Tejo’s ballistic guidance system pressures the enemy to relinquish their ground - or their lives. His targeted strikes keep opponents off balance and under his heel.

## Abilities

### [E] Guided Salvo  (free)

EQUIP an AR targeting system. FIRE to select up to two target locations on the map. ALT FIRE to
launch missiles that autonomously navigate to target locations, detonating repeatedly for damage on
arrival.

### [Q] Special Delivery  (100 cr)

EQUIP a sticky grenade. FIRE to launch. The grenade sticks to the first surface it hits and
explodes, Concussing and dealing damage to any targets caught in the blast. ALT FIRE to launch the
grenade with a single bounce instead.

### [X] Armageddon  (8 ult pts)

EQUIP a tactical strike targeting map. FIRE to select the origin point of the strike. FIRE again to
set the end point and launch the attack, unleashing a wave of lethal damaging explosions along the
strike path. ALT FIRE during map targeting to cancel the origin point.

### [C] Stealth Drone  (200 cr)

EQUIP a stealth drone. FIRE to throw the drone forward, assuming direct control of its movement.
FIRE again to trigger a pulse that Suppresses and Reveals enemies hit.

## Playstyle

Long-range initiator. Guided Salvo provides free, precise denial from across the map. Stealth Drone suppresses defenders before entry. Special Delivery concusses chokepoints without needing line of sight.

## Economy Note

Guided Salvo is free — use it every round to soften defenders. Special Delivery (100) is the cheapest concuss in the game.
