# Breach — Initiator

The bionic Swede Breach fires powerful, targeted kinetic blasts to aggressively clear a path through enemy ground. The damage and disruption he inflicts ensures no fight is ever fair.

## Abilities

### [Q] Flashpoint  (250 cr)

EQUIP a Blinding charge. FIRE the charge to set a fast-acting burst through the wall. The charge
detonates to Blind all players looking at it.

### [E] Fault Line  (free)

EQUIP a Seismic Blast. HOLD FIRE to increase the distance. RELEASE to set off the quake, Concussing
all players in its zone and in a line up to the zone.

### [C] Aftershock  (100 cr)

EQUIP a fusion charge. FIRE the charge to set a slow-acting burst through the wall. The burst does
heavy damage to anyone caught in its area.

### [X] Rolling Thunder  (7 ult pts)

EQUIP a Seismic Charge. FIRE to send a cascading quake through all terrain in a large zone. The
quake Concusses and knocks up anyone caught in it.

## Playstyle

Through-wall utility king. Flashpoint through walls is the safest flash in the game. Fault Line into a site is a free take if opponents aren't off-angles.

## Economy Note

Aftershock (100) is underrated for post-plant. Flashpoint (250) is the must-buy.
