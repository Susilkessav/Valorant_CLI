## Rotation Notes

Three rotating stone doors connect mid to each site; the doors make loud sounds when used. Rotating through mid (Waterfall, Root) is key.

## Tactical Notes

Like Haven, 3 sites stress defender rotations. The rotating doors are game-defining mechanics — timing door sounds reveals incoming rotations.
