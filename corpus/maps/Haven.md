## Rotation Notes

C Long → C Site is fastest CT rotate. Mid Doors connect to both B and A sides. Defenders must watch 3 sites — rotations are the longest in the game.

## Tactical Notes

Only 3-site map. Smokes for Mid Doors and Mid Top are highest priority — cutting off rotations is more important than site smokes. C Long is the longest approach; B is the fastest default.
