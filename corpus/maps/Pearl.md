## Rotation Notes

Mid connects to both A Link and B Link. Top Mid is a critical high-ground position that enables pressure on both sites.

## Tactical Notes

Fully enclosed underwater city map — no elevated or sky-based mechanics. Long corridors on A Main and B Main favor Op. Mid control is essential for splitting the map.
