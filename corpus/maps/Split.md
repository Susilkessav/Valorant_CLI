# Split

**Sites:** A/B

## Callouts

### Attacker Side

Spawn

### A

A Default, A Heaven, A Link, Back, Lobby, Main, Rafters, Ramps, Screens, Sewer, Site, Tower

### B

Alley, B Default, B Heaven, B Main, B Mid, Back, Garage, Link, Lobby, Rafters, Site, Stairs, Tower

### Mid

Bottom, Mail, Mid, Top, Top Mid, Vent

### Defender Side

Spawn

## Rotation Notes

A–B through Mid Top or Vent. Heaven on both sites provides elevated defender advantage.

## Tactical Notes

Heavily defender-sided map. Heaven positions on A and B are almost mandatory to hold. A Ramps is the most contested chokepoint on attack.
