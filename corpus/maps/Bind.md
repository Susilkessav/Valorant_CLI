**Sites:** A/B

### B

### Mid

### Defender Side

Spawn

## Rotation Notes

Bind has no traditional mid — instead, two one-way teleporters connect the halves. Teleporter A exits near B Short; Teleporter B exits near A Short.

## Tactical Notes

Teleporters enable fast rotations but make loud sounds. Timing the teleporter sound is critical intel.
