## Rotation Notes

Mid Bridge is a fast A–B connector. There are no map boundaries — falling off the edges is instant death.

## Tactical Notes

No wall boundaries — certain angles and pushes near edges can result in falling off the map. Vertical peeking from ledges is a unique mechanic.
