# Execute Patterns

## What is an execute
An execute is a coordinated site take where the team commits all or most of its utility at once to overwhelm defenders. It is the opposite of a slow default — speed and simultaneous utility use are the key differentiators.

## Execute structure
1. **Agree on the site** — everyone commits. Split executes are rare and require precise timing.
2. **Utility out simultaneously** — smokes, flashes, concusses all land at the same time. Staggered utility lets defenders recover.
3. **Entry goes immediately** — don't smoke and wait. The smoke is for the push, not for watching.
4. **Trade immediately** — second player must be within one second of the entry fragger.
5. **Plant quickly** — the longer you delay on site, the more time defenders have to rotate.

## A-site execute (general principles)
- Smoke heaven/high ground first — it has line of sight on most A sites.
- Smoke CT or the back of site to prevent a defender from holding the plant.
- Flash the main entry angle the entry fragger is taking (not a smoke angle — they're already blocked).
- Enter main first; one player comes from the secondary entry (catwalk, link, etc.) for a simultaneous split.

## B-site execute (general principles)
- B sites are usually smaller and faster to take with fewer smokes.
- One smoke on CT/back-site, one flash into site is often enough.
- Speed matters more on B — the closer site to CT means faster rotations from defenders.
- Plant toward an off-angle that denies the CT rotate line of sight.

## Post-execute (post-plant)
- Spread across the three exits (main, CT, link/window depending on map).
- One player holds the defuse angle at close range; others hold rotators.
- If an opponent peeks early, trade immediately — don't let them get a free pick.
- Use remaining utility (molly, grenade) on the defuse spot only when you hear defuse start.

## Fake executes
- Commit utility (smoke, flash) on one site, then rotate the entire team to the other site.
- The fake only works if at least 2–3 defenders are already committed to the first site.
- Requires fast rotation — the window between the fake commitment and the real execute is ~10–15 seconds.
- Do not fake if your team is down in credits on utility — you're wasting a limited resource.

## Common execute mistakes
- Staggered utility: smokes land, team waits, flash lands, team waits — defenders recover between each.
- Entry without a trader within 2 seconds.
- Planting in an exposed position instead of behind cover or at an off-angle.
- All five players entering from the same choke — one molly or area-denial ability kills the execute.
